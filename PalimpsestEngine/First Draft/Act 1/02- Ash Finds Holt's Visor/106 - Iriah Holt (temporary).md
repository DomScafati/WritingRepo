Iriah Holt 1
Day 18

I must admit this device we discovered does give me pause.
We woke up a couple of the scientists on Mother's orders.
They spent a week held up in the Engineer's workshop setting up and taking samples from us.

Didn't tell us crawlers anything.
We were just left in the dark.

Early this morning, the scientists, Dr. Braun, Dr. Nero, and Dr. Selaco, just went back into the cryopods without saying anything.
It was... bizarre.

And before any of us could figure out what was going on, Mother called us into the workshop and told us about the Sarcophagus.
When we walked in, it was hooked up to a bunch of bags, I counted 20. 
They were filled with amniotic fluid.
Mother took to calling the alien device the Palimpsest Engine and explained that it...
that it could bring us back.
It was too late for Eddard and Goulding, they were gone since the first week.
But the rest of us were to go back into our private quarters and enter our pods.
We were "templates" she said. The originals.
And this device could keep sending us out there, or some version of us, while we stayed safe.
None of us forgot what happened to the other two, so if we had any reservations, any treacherous thoughts, we stayed silent and did as we were told.
Now as I prepare to go to sleep my thoughts drift back to the sarcophagus' hum.
