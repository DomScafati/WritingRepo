Alix Ward
Day 3

I'm the only one who came back from crawl #2.
Renn and Karys were some of the best crawlers i'd ever seen.
We were all together and went into a some sort of temple that was mostly buried in the ice save for the outer perimeter.
It had some major security but we avoided a couple of sentry turrets that were still active.
Aliens ain't been home for a millennia and their tech is still kicking. 
I'd be impressed if all their shit wasn't trying to kill us every time we took a step.

To get in, I had to rip a bulkhead with my codex, easy enough.
A few of us tech savvy crawlers have taken to using this wrist-mounted decryption device for entering locked doors, and when its useful, its a life saver. 
But now, feels like this temple wanted us to enter so it could eat us alive.

The odd, angular door grinded open and after moving deeper into the building I put together that it was a military complex.
The entrance we came through was some sort of landing pad.
There were long corridors with toppled shipping crates locked tighter than a nun's arsehole.
Figured there would be weapons or something simple we could give to Mother to recycle.
Renn is a strong bloke and popped one open with his crow bar and it was absolutely filled to the brim with these clockwork bugs.
They had brass bodies and pincers like a bloody jagged knife.
Before we could get a word out they swarmed him and tore him apart.
Within seconds, fucking seconds, he was a bloody stump.
We didn't know what to do, so Karys and I each dived into cover.
Neither of us said a word for several agonizing moments.
The clicking of their feet got louder and louder until i felt one on my arm.
I was frozen, sweating ice cubes before Karys blasted it and yelled to run like hell.
We almost made it outside before they overtook us.
One sliced me pretty good, but I faired better than she did.

Two lives of damn good crawlers, the best i'd ever seen.
And all we got for out trouble was a single fucking bug.
We weren't able to recover them.

Mother says we're to go solo from now on to reduce big losses like this. I don't argue.
Probably for the best. There's only so many of us and we have so much work to do.

It should have been me. 
I'm a goddamned coward.
