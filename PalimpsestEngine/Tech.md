# General
## I/O Jack

An **I/O Jack** is a neural interface port that allows users to directly connect to digital systems and enter a shared virtual environment commonly referred to as The Matrix. While interfaced, the operator’s consciousness is partially dissociated from their physical body, rendering them largely unaware of their surroundings—similar to a lucid dream state.

Colloquially, the device is referred to simply as a **“jack.”**

I/O Jacks are ubiquitous civilian technology, considered as commonplace as personal communication devices once were. Most models include an integrated messaging and journaling system with a transmission range of several thousand miles, allowing for both long-distance communication and personal record keeping.
## Terran-Class Ark
A **Terran-Class Ark** is a massive, spherical colony vessel designed for interstellar seeding operations. The ship employs anti-gravity drives for atmospheric and terrestrial maneuvering, as well as miniature wormhole generators for travel between star systems.

Once a candidate world has been fully vetted, an Ark is dispatched to deliver colonists and establish a permanent human presence. Each vessel carries **tens of thousands of cryogenic pods**, allowing passengers to remain in hibernation for decades at a time.

Terran-Class Arks are powered by onboard nuclear reactors, supplying long-term energy for ship operations and early colonial infrastructure after planetfall. Advanced fabrication systems are standard, capable of producing essential equipment and recycling raw or salvaged materials into usable gear. All fabrication and logistics are overseen by the ship’s AI.

## Fabricators
Along with Terran-Class vessels and Cryogenic Hibernation Chambers, **Fabricators** stand among the crowning technological achievements of mankind. Typically overseen by a Director AI, these systems use approved digital schematics to construct physical objects from reclaimed and recycled materials.

Fabricators are capable of producing fully assembled items of low to medium complexity, including tools, weapons, protective equipment, and structural components. While they can manufactureindividual parts for more complex systems, they are incapable of fabricating fully integrated advanced devices. Final assembly, calibration, and system integration must be performed manually. Furthermore the fabricator also cannot create powersources or generate energy itself.

Fabrication is limited to human-designed schematics. Unknown, alien, or partially understood technologies cannot be replicated, even when physically present. As a result, recovered artifacts and components from non-human sources remain irreplaceable.

A defining feature of Fabricators is their ability to process recycled input materials. Through an energy-intensive conversion process, the system can alter the molecular structure of available matter to meet schematic requirements. This process is highly inefficient, producing significant waste and material loss, and places strict limits on output volume. Certain transformations require disproportionate energy expenditure and are rarely authorized.

Due to power constraints and material scarcity, Fabricator use is heavily rationed. Fabrication queues, schematic approval, and resource prioritization are managed centrally, with mission-critical repairs taking precedence over individual equipment requests. As a result, while Fabricators ensure long-term operational survival, they cannot replace scarcity.
## Croygenic Hybernation Chamber
Often reffered to as a "Cryopod" or "Fridge", this device uses a complex system to freeze the user in suspended stasis, halting cell aging and metabolism to a tiny fraction of it's normal rate. This puts the user into hybernation where they can sleep hundreds of years with no signs of aging. 

The down side to this device is the strain on neural pathways. Even the most resilient people face some degree of "Cryo Sickness" after awakening. This Cryo Sickness gets worse based on the amount of time the user has been hibernating and often results in memory loss, hallucinations, and headaches. While it is not usually fatal, the longest currently observed hibernation has only been 200 earth years and further experimentation must be done.
### Crew Structure
- **Director AI**  
    Rather than a traditional captain, the Ark is governed by a director-class artificial intelligence responsible for all critical systems and long-term mission planning.
- **Maintenance Automata**  
    Simple autonomous units that perform routine repairs and manage cryopod systems while colonists remain in stasis.
- **Engineers**  
    A broad classification encompassing all STEM-trained colonists. Engineers may be awakened early to perform complex maintenance, fabrication, or problem-solving beyond the capabilities of the automata.
- **Colonists**  
    Passengers whose primary function is to populate and sustain the new settlement. Most assume massive financial debt to secure passage, which must be repaid through labor after arrival. This debt is genetically encoded and can be inherited by their descendants.
- **Crawlers**  
    Professional mercenaries tasked with ensuring early colony survival. Their responsibilities include hostile fauna suppression, pathfinding, hunting, and—most commonly—scavenging alien or pre-existing technology.
    
# Crawler Gear
Crawlers are issued a standardized loadout for all major expeditions. They train extensively with this equipment, as replacements can be readily fabricated aboard a Terran-Class Ark.

## Perception Visor
The **Perception Visor** is standard-issue Crawler field gear. It replaces earlier modular systems by consolidating **life support, sensory mediation, and data interpretation** into a single integrated unit.

In common usage, it is referred to simply as the **“Visor.”**

The Visor is a sealed, tight-fitting helmet designed to be worn either independently or integrated directly into heavier EVA suits. Once donned, it connects to the user’s I/O Jack and becomes the primary intermediary between the Crawler and their environment.

From the exterior, the Visor presents a smooth, continuous faceplate. Rather than exposing the wearer’s features, the faceplate displays a **holographic, non-specific facial projection**, generated in real time.

### Core Functions
- **Atmospheric Filtration & Breathing Support**  
    The Visor provides continuous air filtration and oxygen regulation suitable for marginally breathable or chemically hostile environments. It replaces the need for a separate rebreather system.
- **Environmental Interpretation**  
    Raw sensory input is filtered, enhanced, and reframed before reaching the user. Lighting conditions, atmospheric interference, and particulate distortion are dynamically corrected.
- **Physiological Monitoring**  
    The Visor continuously tracks heart rate, oxygen saturation, stress responses, and trauma indicators. Severe anomalies are flagged automatically.
- **Spatial Awareness Overlay**  
    Displays relative positioning of mapped structures, known hazards, and previously marked points of interest.
- **Object & Threat Analysis**  
    Known flora, fauna, and technology are passively scanned and annotated when reference data exists.
- **Automatic Transcription**  
    All field activity is recorded and transcribed through the Visor, including spoken dialogue and environmental audio. Manual deactivation is not supported during active deployment.

### Operational Notes
- Removal of the Visor during a surface operation is strongly discouraged.
- Crawlers report disorientation, panic, and sensory overload when separated from the Visor for extended periods. This is what is known as "System Addiction" and curing it requires careful weening.
## Railgun Revolver
A compact electromagnetic sidearm that fires solid metal projectiles. Despite its size, the weapon is capable of penetrating reinforced armor. Favored for reliability and ease of fabrication.
## High-Frequency Blade
A fixed-blade knife engineered to vibrate at extremely high frequencies, allowing it to cut through most conventional materials. Used as both a utility tool and a last-resort weapon.
