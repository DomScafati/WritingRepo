### **Epistolary Angles & Document Sources**

- **Stalker Mission Logs**  
    First-person audio or text entries from past Stalkers, showing gradual psychological degradation across multiple deaths.
- **Post-Mortem / Resurrection Reports**  
    Clinical system summaries noting “successful revival” while subtly omitting cognitive decline.
- **Incident & Recovery Reports**  
    Official records declaring Stalkers KIA
- **Psych Evaluations & Fitness Reviews**  
    Early confidence and professionalism giving way to obsession, paranoia, or fixation on survival.
- **MOTHER XIII Status Briefings**  
    Neutral, procedural updates that reveal the system’s indifference to human cost.
- **Unattributed or Fragmented Surface Logs**  
    Disjointed notes left by near-trog Stalkers—half-legible, repetitive, or ritualistic.
- **Final Messages to the Colonists (Never Sent)**  
    Personal recordings meant to justify the mission, exposing fear, doubt, or regret.
