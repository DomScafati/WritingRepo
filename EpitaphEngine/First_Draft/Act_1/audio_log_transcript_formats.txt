Casual and personal. A stripped-down format where the speaker is identified once at the top. No timestamps. Emphasizes mood and atmosphere.

Device: Wristband recorder (voice-triggered)

Mirelle:
I’m starting to forget what my own voice sounds like.

Same pitch. Same stutter.  
Same exact voice.

I rounded the corner.  
No one there.

[soft hum of atmosphere controls]
[heartbeat spike – briefly detected by mic]

Either way… I think I just answered myself.

[log ends]
