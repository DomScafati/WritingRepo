## **Maintenance / Technical Logs**

1. **Burnt Out** – An exhausted engineer logs yet another repair on a system that’s “held together by hope and tape.” Mentions using stolen parts from the medical bay.
    
2. **Ghost Current** – Technician swears there’s power running through a section of the ship that’s been disconnected for months.
    
3. **Patch Job** – Emergency hull breach patch report… while the breach is still slowly widening in the background.
    
4. **Air Mix 3B** – Life support tech warns about “weird sweet smell” in air mix, swears it’s not from his cooking.
