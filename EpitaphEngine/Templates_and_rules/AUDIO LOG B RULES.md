## AUDIO LOG FORMAT B RULES –

### **1. Nature & Purpose**

- This log type is **not created by standard ship recorders** — it’s the output of an **embedded wetware recorder** inside someone’s neural chip or cybernetic implant.
- The device activates **without the subject’s knowledge** whenever certain neurological patterns spike — rage, fear, obsessive focus.
- Most often tied to _Maddax_ (the murderer’s consciousness inside Mahoney), but could also capture environmental fragments when the “Other” is active.

---

### **2. Header Structure**

- Always looks different from standard logs, to cue the reader it’s _off the books_.
- Example:
    
    ```
    :: SYSTEM LOG: UNAUTHORIZED RECORDING DETECTED ::
    :: ID: [REDACTED] ::
    :: ORIGIN TRACE: Wetware Echo Loop – Sector C ::
    :: STATUS: Data Integrity Compromised (██% corrupted) ::
    ```
- Keep **double colons** and all-caps for the “computer” feel.
- Corruption percentage can vary for tension (sometimes 12%, sometimes 78%).

---

### **3. Opening & Closing Markers**

- Start with:
    ```
    <<AUDIO BEGINS>>
    ```
- End with:
    ```
    <<END SEGMENT>>
    ```
- These are part of the format’s “cold” machine voice — never casual.

---

### **4. Voice & Dialogue**

- Speech is **fragmented, poetic, sometimes looping** — not always full sentences.
- May use **layered descriptions** to show distortion:
    - `**[heavily layered voice]**`
    - `**[whisper overlaps with itself]**`
- Content often blurs lines between present and memory, self and other.

---

### **5. Sound Cues**

- More stylized and abstract than in Format A:
    - `[glitch-spike]`
    - `[loop begins repeating]`
    - `[low bass rumble]`
- Can mix literal sounds with _impressionistic_ ones:
    - `[teeth grinding on metal]`
    - `[something wet in the walls]`

---

### **6. Visual Corruption**

- Words can be **struck through, glitched, or replaced with ███** to imply missing or corrupted data:
    - `she ███ me in the dark.`
    - `I [error::fragment] her face.`
- Occasionally insert repeated words or stutters for a broken-record effect.

---

### **7. Narrative Role**

- Use sparingly — these should feel _rare and invasive_.
- Reader should slowly piece together that these are **from inside Mahoney’s own head** — but it’s never outright confirmed.
- Can act as the “monster’s POV,” a memory bleed, or a warning of what’s coming.

---
### **Example**

```
:: SYSTEM LOG: UNAUTHORIZED RECORDING DETECTED ::
:: ID: [REDACTED] ::
:: ORIGIN TRACE: Wetware Echo Loop – Corridor E ::
:: STATUS: Data Integrity Compromised (32% corrupted) ::

<<AUDIO BEGINS>>
**[heavily layered voice]**
She walked past me again.
Didn’t even look.
Like I was air.
Like I was *hers.*

We used to share the same spine.
Now she wears me like a ghost.

**[glitch-spike – loop begins repeating]**
you left / you left / you left / you left me—

**[silence – whisper, clear]**
She’ll hear me this time.
I’ll use her voice.

<<END SEGMENT>>
```
