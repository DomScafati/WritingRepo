This was Darkworth's only gift that she could give Amara, exemption from the Mortifier units and a swift death in battle. A chance to go out with a roar and not a quiet, feeble wimper. As they walked out of the room, Amara's head tilted slightly towards the Inquisitor. Her eyes, still grim and bathed in darkness, shot Emilia a look of gratitude. She returned one of sorrow before walking in the other direction.
