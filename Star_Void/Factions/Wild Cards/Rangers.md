Overview
These are highly trained survivalists, explorers, frontiersmen and monster-hunters who have high-tech weaponry, They-bone armor and have been imbued with Marrow. They operate outside the jurisdiction of most forms of human governance and are allowed to go wherever and do whatever they deem necessary to map out the galaxy, find alien artifacts, find life-viable planets and destroy hostile fauna.

## Standard Appearance:
- Device to a pocket dimension, holds their equipment, about as big as a coffin
- Firearm
- Bone Blade
- ivory They-bone armor that covers large portions, but not all, of their body.
- face mask that covers up to their nose, looks like skull
- A skull pauldron of a major monster they have killed
