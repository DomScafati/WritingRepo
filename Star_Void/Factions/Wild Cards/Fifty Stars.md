An terrorist organization formed after the Galactic Civil War with the goal of killing the leaders of the new STAR government and to rebuild it in their ideal image, a version of the government before the massive galactic expansion and the subsequent civil war.

this group contacts people from any of the core races from both STAR and VOID who have issues with the government or the mega-corporations. This decentralized group contacts individuals via phone call and subtly coerces them with cryptic messages to kill people.

inspired by hotline miami and the fifty blessings organization.
