## Overview
*(High-level description of who they are, their role in society, and any notable traits.)*  
**Primary Faction:** Jackalope
**Allies:** [[STAR Overview|All STAR factions]], [[Rangers]]
**Enemies:** [[VOID Overview|All VOID factions]], [[Wendigos]], [[Skinwalkers]]
**Compatible Species**: Jackalopes only
**Summary:** The Dust Preachers are both Bard and Law Bringer of the frontier. They are traveling storytellers and moral arbiters. They teach frontier law through stories and cautionary tales. These philosopher sheriffs settle disputes, preserve peace and deliver eulogies to the fallen. Though they often prefer to prevent violence through their resonance-based ability "The Voice" to momentarily force a command, they are also known for being exceptionally lucky and mean ass gunslingers.

## Combat
*(Describe how they fight — tactics, weapons, style, doctrine.)*  
**Specialization:** They utilize lightning quick reflexes and precise aim to instantly neutralize any opposition. Being extraordinarily lucky, they prefer to wear little armor, as it only hinders their agility and natural athleticism. They are also known to utilize _the Voice_ as much as in combat as outside of it.
**Common Weapons:** 
- [[Rail Revolver]]
**Tactics & Doctrine:** Preachers are rogues, preferring to travel light and keeping the wind to their backs. They prefer a fair fight, but will use any dirty trick at their disposal, including hidden cybernetics, if it is a means to serve out justice. They enforce a vague moral justice system that warns agains malice, use of marrow and violence against the innocent. 
**Unique Traits:**
- Considered lucky, even among jackalopes
- Utilize an advanced resonance ability _the Voice_, to command opponents, though it is less effective against those will equal or higher resonance than their own.
- Lightning fast draw and reflexes
- Use light cybernetics
