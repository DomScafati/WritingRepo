## Government
The government, officially rules over most of the galaxy including the VOID worlds before their rebellion. 

This is a government spread too thin, across too many worlds, so most of the galactic expansion was left to corporations to help with. This of course gave the private sector too much power, and through systems like Gene Debt and Indentured Servitude, lead to a great deal of strife across the people of the stars.

When STAR announced to the planets on the outer reach that they would be setting up more government presence in these worlds and dismantling the brutal sacrifices and slavery culture, this lead to VOID's seccession.
## Religion
Generally, religion and spiritualism is accepted widely throughout the STAR worlds, but is much more localized and secular. Typically, the different worlds believe in The Great Spirit, a metaphysical being unlike They, who are widely considered to be the opposite of The Great Spirit, ie., Dark Old Gods.

The military fights its battles with the use of less, more commando-esc groups. instead of large firing lines, STAR opts to focus more on training soldiers as much as they can and then outfitting them with cutting edge technology like automata squadmates (Thunder Brothers), targeting cybernetics and the coveted [[Rail Rifle]]. The reality it, even though STAR would love to utilized numbers to their advantage, it isn't possible to match VOID in this way due to logistical reasons, which is why they focus more on quality.

Humans are the most populous species, valued for their adaptability. Though **inert to resonance**, they make up for this weakness by utilizing the aforementioned cybernetics and advanced technology. They also have a higher success rate of becoming [[Rangers]] due to their ability to survive marrow at a higher rate. [[Rangers]] however, do not directly identify as a force of STAR and act independently.
