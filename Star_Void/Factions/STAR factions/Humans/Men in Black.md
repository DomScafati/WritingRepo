## Overview
*(High-level description of who they are, their role in society, and any notable traits.)*  
**Primary Faction:** STAR Humans
**Allies:** [[The President]], [[Mega-Corps]]
**Enemies:** [[VOID Overview|All VOID Factions]], rogue marrowers, dissidents
**Compatible Species**: [[MK Ultra]] engineered humans only
**Summary:** The Men in Black are a clandestine group of transhumans created under project [[MK Ultra]]. Their existence is officially denied by STAR and any other group that may know about them. They operate in groups of two called Dyads, they investigate sightings of the ever elusive [[Star People]], recover alien relics and neutralize individuals who's power might destabilize the government.

Each agent is vat-grown from a [[MK Ultra]]'s hybrid genome - spliced with dormant [[Star People]] DNA to give them terrifying psionic power and a perfect memory. They're sociopaths that care for few things other than their partner, [[The President]], and the idea of Law and Order.

To acknowledge their existence would invite public panic — proof that the government still traffics with Star People.

## Combat
*(Describe how they fight — tactics, weapons, style, doctrine.)*  
**Specialization:** Precision assassinations, psionic combat, covert infiltraion, psycological warfare. 
**Common Weapons:** 
- [[Black Briefcase]]
- [[Plasma Pistol]]
**Tactics & Doctrine:** They always work in pairs. They strike with surgical efficiency and remove any evidence they were ever there.
**Unique Traits:**
- Twin-linked psionic resonance: each member of a Dyad can feel each others thoughts, pain and emotion.
- Their psionics are precise but non-lethal — illusions, memory extraction, hypnotic command. Their strength lies not in destruction, but control.
- Inhumanly calm demeanor- immune to fear responses and broken morale.

## Societal Importance
*(Explain their position within culture and politics — how civilians, STAR, and others view them.)*  
**Civil Standing:** Officially non-existent. Among colonists, they are a campfire horror story.
**Relationship with STAR:** Only subordinate to [[The President]] himself, Dyads act with total operational autonomy. Their clearance supersedes even High Command. To cross them is to vanish.
**Economic/Political Role:** Silences corporate scandals, political assassinations for dissenters, locate [[Star People]] and their technology and return to government for study, maintain the stability of STAR by any means necessary.
**Inter-faction Relations:**
- The [[Rangers]] and [[Minute Men]] that do remember them, dislike them. Seeing them as moral parasites.
- Welcomed by [[Mega-Corps]], seeing their value.
- Hated by [[Crusaders]] who see they as soulless abominations.

## Ethos
*(Beliefs, values, and behavioral codes that define them.)*  
"Always watching."

- **Core Tenets:**  
  1. Secrecy: Truth is the enemy of order.
  2. Symmetry: Left sees, right acts.
  3. Obedience: [[The President]] is the arbiter of morality. 
**Cultural Practices:** 
- Dyads are raised from "birth" with their partner and around adolescence their telepathic bond reaches maturity.
- Dyads share a single number, prefixed by Left or Right respectively. (Left 78, Right 78).
- Dyads lose the desire to live without their partner and will usually self-terminate if one expires before the other.
**Moral Compass:** Utilitarian and obedient by genetic design, they are detached, but not amoral. They believe in law and order, the chain of command and the idea that finding more of their ancestors, [[Star People]], will result in the survival of humanity. While they have no faith in The Great Spirit, one could argue their faith in law, order, and [[The President]] is it's own religion.
