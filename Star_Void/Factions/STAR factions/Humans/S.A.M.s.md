## Overview
*(High-level description of who they are, their role in society, and any notable traits.)*  
**Primary Faction:** STAR Humans
**Allies:** [[STAR Overview|All Star Factions]], [[Rangers]]
**Enemies:** [[VOID Overview| All Void Factions]], [[Skinwalkers]], Monsters
**Compatible Species**: Humans, Jackalopes, Hidefolk
**Summary:** Striding Artillery Mechs- "Uncle Sams" are 30-50 foot tall, heavily armored, bipedal mechanized units, these highly trained pilots provide support to the [[Minute Men]]. Through heavy machine gunning, artillery fire, or just being a walking distraction, the SAMs are called in when a massive fight is about to happen. On top of being a dangerous and expensive asset, STAR has also trained the pilots and designed the machine to be a propaganda tool, being a walking reminder of the "freedom" the soldiers are fighting for.

## Combat
*(Describe how they fight — tactics, weapons, style, doctrine.)*  
**Specialization:** These units specialize in heavy weapons, artillery and morale support. Their strategies are versitile; sometimes they draw fire from heavy weapon's nests, tamed monsters and Marrowers, other times they attack from great distance behind the front line with their massive weapons.
**Common Weapons:** 
- [[Gatling Rail Cannons]]
- [[Collapsed Matter Rifle]]
- [[Fusion Cannon]]
- [[Fusion Saber]]
- [[Fission Grenade]]
**Tactics & Doctrine:** Different pilots have different preferences on how they kit their SAM, but they almost always deploy in crucial battles, being massively important assets, STAR doesn't deploy them unless the risk of damage or pilot casualty is outweighed by the potential victory. When they walk into the battle front, their built-in speakers blare music and speech to inspire allies and disparage enemies.
**Unique Traits:** giant robots with the highest budget in the Allied Republics.
