## Overview
*(High-level description of who they are, their role in society, and any notable traits.)*  
**Primary Faction:** STAR Humans
**Allies:** [[STAR Overview|All STAR factions]], [[Rangers]], [[Mega-Corps]]
**Enemies:** [[VOID Overview|All VOID factions]], Monsters, [[Skinwalkers]]
**Compatible Species**: Humans, Jackalopes, Hidefolk
**Summary:** The Minute Men are the backbone of STAR’s military might — an army of citizen-soldiers and professional veterans united under one banner. They are neither zealots nor mercenaries, but disciplined defenders of civilization’s fragile edge. Their name honors the ancient militias of Old Earth, and the fact that orbital deployment protocols can drop them anywhere in under sixty seconds.
  
Minute Men are renowned for their use of _smart weaponry, powered exosuits,_ and _rail-based kinetic arms._ Each platoon operates with battlefield AI coordination and heavy mechanized support, trading mysticism for machine precision. Where [[Crusaders]] rely on holy fervor and marrow mutations, the Minute Men rely on training, teamwork, and technology — the three pillars of STAR warfare.

## Combat
*(Describe how they fight — tactics, weapons, style, doctrine.)*  
**Specialization:** Combined-arms mechanized warfare and tactical flexibility. While inert to resonance and unable to use Marrow safely, they compensate with predictive targeting networks, drone relays, and cybernetic augmentation.
**Common Weapons:**
- [[Rail Rifle]]
**Tactics & Doctrine:**  Minute Men doctrine emphasizes _coordination over heroics._ Squads operate in quintet of specialists: two rifleman, support, scout and ai drone "The Thunder Brother". They are often placed in this modularized squads to bolster morale and deepen relationships. They favor drop-pod strikes, heavy orbital support, and entrenched mechanized positions — fighting wars with precision and patience rather than zeal. 
**Unique Traits:** 
- Cybernetic compatibility standard across all ranks
- Combat AI cohesion
- Rely heavily on AI Drone and mechanized support

## Societal Importance
*(Explain their position within culture and politics — how civilians, STAR, and others view them.)*  
**Civil Standing:** Typically respected, but not worshipped.
**Relationship with STAR:** Fully incorporated int STAR's command structure. While some local militia's exist, all operate under standardized protocols.
**Economic/Political Role:** Many individuals join to reduce their genetic debt accrued by themselves or their ancestors from [[Mega-Corps]]. 
**Inter-faction Relations:**
- Respectful rivalry with [[Rangers]] (Frontier heroes vs disciplined army)
- Works often with [[Frontier Watchmen]] on operations.
- Much of their equipment is built by [[Moonshine Alchemists]].

## Ethos
*(Beliefs, values, and behavioral codes that define them.)*  
"You are five fingers, we are a hand."

**Core Tenets:**  
  1. Readiness: The agents of They are always near. Be ready.
  2. Precision: Strike with discipline, not emotion.
  3. Unity: Work together, survive together.
**Cultural Practices:** 
- “The Clean Arm” — ritual removal and replacement of dominant arm with cybernetic augment upon enlistment.
- Taboos against altering facial cybernetics (faces remain human as a mark of individuality).
- When the entire squad sees the humanity in their fifth member, the AI Drone, it is seen as a beautiful moment of full cohesion. The drone ceases to be a tool and squad welcomes it as their "Thunder Brother."
**Moral Compass:** As an institution, they are pragmatic humanists. They respect The Great Spirit but do not worship — to them, divinity lies in human perseverance and the preservation of civilization. Though not officially recognized, it is a military made of people, and every person will vary, many will whisper a silent prayer to The Great Spirit just before battle.
