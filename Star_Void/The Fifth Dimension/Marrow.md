### **Overview**

When one of They dies, either by fighting each other or decay from the universe's past, some of Their organic material can still be salvaged. However, this isn't flesh in the human sense — this never belonged to our dimension. Among these salvageable remains, the most potent are the **Bone Marrow** and **Brain Matter** — semi-living substances that hum with ethereal energy.

Through horrific experimentation, it was discovered that these cells can be refined and injected into living beings. Small doses of this tissue can overwrite biological limits for a short time. Each refined dose is called a **Marrow** Hypo. The process is an unholy amalgamation of science and sacrilege — a mix of chemistry and desperation.

No two doses are the same. Each carries fragments of alien will, slivers of what They are. Every injection is a conversation with a god that is already dead.

Over time, it was found that Marrow does more than temporarily distort the body — it _rewrites it_. A single dose permanently alters the user's biology, grafting a new function or capability into the nervous system. The ability remains dormant after the initial surge, lying inert until fueled by **Autogametes**, the blank reproductive cells of They.  
In this way, Marrow is _knowledge_ — a learned mutation — and Gametes are _fuel_, the divine medium that lets the power ignite again.

There is no known upper limit to how many different Marrow strains one being can survive. However, each new graft exponentially increases the likelihood of failure — organ collapse, mutation, or death. Few risk more than one or two; none have survived more than six.

### **Body Parts**

**Bone Marrow** (Spells)– volatile source of permanent abilities; each dose seeds a new mutation into the host’s biology.  

**Monogonad / Monogamete** (Mana) – self-fertilizing reproductive cell; blank biological energy used to fuel Marrow abilities safely.

**Bone** – ultra-light and nearly unbreakable material; used for armor, implants, and ship hulls.  

**Brain Matter** – psionic catalyst; capable of awakening terrifying cognitive powers, but with a near-absolute mortality rate.  

---

### **Accessibility**

Marrow Hypos flood the user's nervous system with reactive proteins and alien cells that distort electrical and chemical signaling. The body becomes a conduit for phenomena it was never built to host.

**Duration:** Minutes to hours of altered power before the body's immune system begins to reject it. Abilities learned through Marrow remain indefinitely but can only be reactivated through Gamete infusion.

**Unpredictability:** Even the most stable strains are prone to unwanted side-effects. A dose of Fire Marrow may produce pyrokinesis — or completely immolate the user.

**Rarity:** Only one corpse of They has ever been found, and most of its remains have already been devoured by the primordial beings. The scarcity of viable tissue drives empires, cults, and corporations to slaughter each other for the smallest fragment.

**Autogametes:** Though safer and widely used, Gametes are extraordinarily expensive. The cells burn cleanly and completely, leaving no residue, but are nonrenewable — they must be harvested from the Autogonads of They, or cultured from living samples under strict containment.

**Brain Matter:** Far more powerful and far deadlier than Marrow. The psionic enhancements it offers are incomparable — direct communion with alien consciousness. Humans who survive injection will make Star Peoples psionics look like child's play and will make other Marrowjacks look like pushovers. However, the mortality rate exceeds ninety-nine percent. Those who survive are never the same. Their will survives strongest in the brain, and the human mind is rarely strong enough to contain it.

---

### **Cost**

**Physical Degradation:**
- Rapid mutation, organ failure, and necrosis affect nearly all users over time.
- Each additional Marrow graft multiplies instability; cells from different They tissues often reject or consume each other.

**Neurological:**
- Euphoria, chromesthesia, and heightened pain tolerance during use.
- Post-use hallucinations, fugue states, or total identity loss.
- Long-term users report hearing voices, likely remnants of the dead god’s consciousness.

**Spiritual and Existential:**

- Each injection invites small amounts of They into the user's body, eroding the personality of the user.
- Overuse can result in total mental blankness or, in rare cases, the birth of a living vessel through which They reawaken.


---

### **Extraction & Processing**

- **Scale:** A single They corpse spanning dozens of light-years means parts are spatially enormous and dispersed. Harvesting requires fleets, long expeditions, salvage crews, and on-site stabilizers.

- **Hazards:** Environmental anomalies (radiation, time dilation, resonance storms), guardian phenomena (defensive fields, parasitic organisms), bureaucratic warfare (claim wars), and ethical uproar.

- **Processing:** Raw tissue must be cryo-stabilized, de-resonanced, or chemically fractionated. Labs use combinatory tech: nanofilters, resonance dampers, and stabilizers to suppress cellular awareness. Each stage opens failure modes — contamination, runaway reaction, or spontaneous reanimation.

- **Transport:** Moving parts across light-years invites theft, scavengers, or “marrow storms” (leaks producing localized biological distortion).                                                                                                  
### **Rejection and Compatibility**

Only a fraction of humanity can survive a single dose of Marrow.  
Those individuals possess **latent resonance potential** — a rare genetic quirk where trace _Choir Node_-like organelles or adaptive proteins form during embryonic development.

| Human Type            | Description                                                          | Marrow Reaction                                                                                     |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Inert**             | No natural resonance.                                                | Marrow attacks the host at the cellular level; systemic failure or violent mutation within minutes. |
| **Low Resonant**      | Rare carriers of pseudo-Choir Nodes or unstable resonance potential. | Partial integration; unpredictable side effects, survival possible with skill or luck.              |
| **Moderate Resonant** | True “Marrow-touched.” Cells can harmonize with synthetic resonance. | Controlled usage possible; still subject to degradation over time.                                  |

Each **subsequent injection** increases the body’s resonance amplitude — but also destabilizes the pattern.  
Too much, and the signal overwhelms the host, leading to rejection and death.

### **Marrow is a Forced, Louder Resonance**
Mortals with _Resonant Biology_  possess _Choir Nodes_ that hum in tune with the Fifth Dimension through evolution and instinct. Marrow **hijacks** that process — artificially imposing resonance onto inert human tissue. It’s not harmonizing; it’s brute-forcing a signal through a medium that wasn’t built to sing.

Natural resonance is quiet and stable — like background radiation. Marrow drastically **amplifies the waveform**, pushing biological systems to scream into the Fifth Dimension. That volume grants power, but it also draws attention and destabilizes the host.

The user becomes a beacon — not a participant in the field, but a flare of unnatural signal. It bends local physics violently, burns out cells, and risks waking fragments of They.

Natural resonance = _music_. Marrow = _feedback._ One is a song; the other is a shriek of static that still carries melody for a moment before the speaker blows.
