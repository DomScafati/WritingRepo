### **Overview**

Many fauna in the universe possess abilities most would consider supernatural. Within these creatures’ cells exists an organelle known as the **Choir Node** — a structure that constantly phases in and out of the **Fifth Dimension**, making it nearly impossible to observe directly.

Because of this interdimensional flicker, these organisms emit a **causal wavelength** — a background radiation that resonates faintly with **They**, the Fifth-Dimensional gods.

This radiation effectively turns the organism into a **living antenna**, converting microscopic resonance into macroscopic effects.

---

### **Resonant Mechanics**

Think of _Choir Node_ as **biological radio receptors** for a cosmic signal.  
Each organism’s unique resonance determines the scope and risk of its abilities.

- **Frequency** → Determines which aspect of a God they mirror.
- **Density** → Determines the overall strength of the effect.
- **Stability** → Determines whether the host survives long-term exposure.

---

### **Resonance ≠ Channeling**

Resonant organisms are not _drawing_ power from They.  
Rather, they are **harmonizing** with lingering emissions from the Gods’ own causal wavelengths.

A tiny fraction of alignment yields only a _fraction of a fraction_ of divine capability — enough to subtly bend local physics, never enough to rival a God.

### **Resonance Classification**

| Level                  | Description                                                                                           | Examples                                                                          |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Inert**              | Normal lifeform with no _Choir Node_.                                                                 | Most fauna and flora.                                                             |

| **Moderate Resonance** | Noticeable, controllable effects under stress or instinct.                                            | (e.g., Mothinid time-glide, human Marrow users — optional entry to expand later.) |
| **High Resonance**     | Rare, unstable mutations or engineered organisms capable of destructive or reality-bending phenomena. | Marrow Mutants                                                                    |

---

### **The Human Condition**

Humans are, by default, **Inert species** — they lack natural _Choir Nodes_ and therefore emit no Fifth-dimensional resonance.  
To the Fifth Wave, a human is silent.

That silence is why **Marrow** was invented.  

Marrow is a **biogenic slurry** derived from processed _Choir Nodes_ — most famously, from harvested tissue of the fallen God’s corpse.  
When injected, it temporarily _forces_ a resonance pattern on the user’s cells, tricking the body into mimicking a low-frequency signal within the Fifth Dimension.
