Any bladed weapon created from the bone of They. Every Ranger is given one upon their first kill of a cryptid. This is seen universally as a ranger weapons, and any non-ranger with one will almost certainly enrage one.

Fashioned based off of the tribe the ranger hails from, can be in many different manner of blade including:
- Hatchet / Tomahawk
- Saber
- Hunting Knife
- Dagger
- Bone-tooth Club
- Spear
